Hikking on tenerife with gregorio´s Wanderfamily

"We have been guiding our guests for 46 years to known and unknown places on the island of Tenerife."

Gregorio is a true "native" of Tenerife and a passionate hiker on his island. In 1972, Gregorio and his whole family began on Tenerife with guided tours for tourists. For this reason, Gregorio can be the only native hiking guide in Tenerife of himself and his family. In Puerto de la Cruz in the north of the island, there is hardly anyone who does not know Gregorio and his traveling family. Even among hiking enthusiasts who visit Tenerife, the name is well known. Today, the migrant family is continued by Victor, the son of Gregorio, who brings the tradition of the Wanderfamily into a new era.
